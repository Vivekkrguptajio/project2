About Us

Nurturing Excellence in Pharmaceutical Sciences

Founded in 1998, Krishna Institute of Pharmacy is a premier institution dedicated to pharmaceutical education and research. Approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and affiliated to a recognized university, we provide a holistic learning environment that blends academic rigour with practical exposure.

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry connections ensure that our graduates are well-equipped to lead in the ever-evolving world of pharmacy and healthcare.
